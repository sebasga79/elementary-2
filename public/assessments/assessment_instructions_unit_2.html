Unit 2 Final Assessments: Finding Your Place

In these final assessment tasks, you will apply the grammar topics (How Much/Many & Countable/Uncountable Nouns) and vocabulary from this unit in authentic, real-world contexts.

Note: Your teacher will evaluate your natural use of language, participation, and clarity of communication.

🎯 Final Tasks for Unit 2

Complete these activities to demonstrate your mastery of the unit's objectives:

"Find Your Perfect Home" Presentation

Present and advertise a home using quantifier expressions, comparing it to other options.

Neighborhood Improvement Plan

Analyze a real neighborhood and propose improvements using quantity expressions.

Roommate Compatibility Interview

Write interview questions using "how much" and "how many" to find the perfect roommate.
